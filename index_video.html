视频：刻舟求剑的故事

  • A man stood in the boat with a sword in his hand,looking proud.
  • The boat is driving in the river,and suddenly this man's sword falls into the water.
  • The man hurriedly carved a mark on the side of the boat where the sword fell into the water.
  • The boat continued to move,and when it reached the shore,the man went into the water to look for the sword at the side of the boat according to the mark.
  • The man groped around in the water,his face puzzled,but he could not find the sword.
